ICFAI FC Shines at Inter-University NE Zone Tournament 2023-24

20 Players Represent ICFAI FC at RGU, Assam

In a remarkable achievement for the club, 20 talented players from ICFAI FC participated in the prestigious Inter-University North East Zone Football Tournament 2023-24, held at Royal Global University (RGU), Assam.

Debut for ICFAI FC Girls Team in TFA Women's Football League

First-Ever Participation in Tripura’s Premier Women's League – 2023-24

Making history for the club and the state, the ICFAI FC Girls Team participated for the first time ever in the Women's Football League 2023-24, organized by the Tripura Football Association (TFA). The tournament, held in Tripura, marked a significant milestone in the journey of women's football at ICFAI.
